My mom

Fatima is the birthplace of Oghagbon Moses, a Nigerian artist who has spent nearly three decades painting Argungu, the emirate where she was born. Oghagbon has developed a strong connection to Argungu and has been visiting the area since 1999. Oghagbon is a full-time artist based in Lagos and Argungu. His work is well-known in Nigeria and is featured in both public and private collections. He has had four solo exhibitions and more than 40 group exhibitions. His work has been reviewed in magazines, newspapers, and online.

In 2006, Oghagbon was awarded the Ford Foundation/Terra Kulture grant, which allowed him to display some of his tour project collections. He is a member of the Society of Nigerian Artists (SNA), Quatrain, and Ayobo Art Colony (AAC). He also founded the Argungu Series project documentation/representations.
